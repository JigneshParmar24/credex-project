# Economics

BurnRate is fundamentally a lead-generation and qualification tool for Credex rather than a standalone SaaS business. The economics therefore depend on whether the tool can acquire high-intent AI-spending startups cheaply enough and convert a small percentage of them into infrastructure credit buyers.

---

# 1. What is a converted lead worth to Credex?

Assumption:
Credex makes money by selling discounted AI credits and infrastructure contracts to startups.

Estimated average customer profile:
- 8–25 person AI startup
- Monthly AI spend: $2,000–$15,000
- Credex margin on negotiated infrastructure resale or referral: ~10–15%

Conservative estimate:
- Average managed AI spend/customer: $4,000/month
- Credex effective margin: 12%

So:

```txt
$4,000 × 12% = $480/month gross profit
